Welcome to Joshua Ford's Portfolio.
This GitHub portfolio website was created as a team effort between Joshua Ford and Mary Rose O'Donnell. The project was conceived of and designed as a Capstone Project for the Digital Narrative and Intereactive Design major at the University of Pittsburgh. By collaborating with our peers and Dr. Ahmed Ibrahim of Pitt's Department of Informatics and Networked Systems in our Team-Based Capstone Project Course, we were able to create the website you see now.
We were also joined by Jennifer Welton, Director of Corporate Relations at the University of Pittsburgh, who advised us on the design and function of the site in regards to the employers that we will send it to.

DNID

You may wonder what our major of Digital Narrative and Interactive Design means, and how it is a benefit to you.
To begin with, this major was created as a fusion between the narrative and media studies of the English Department and the coding, development, and human-computer interactions focused on in the School of Computing and Information. Students in this major complete training in several fields, including Writing, Composition, Computer Science, and Information Science.
By taking this major, we and our peers have become experienced in writing with human interaction and experience in mind.
